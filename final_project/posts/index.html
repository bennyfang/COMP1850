5 Useful Resources for Japanese Foods and Blogs

Link Description
Ramen Adventures A popular ramen blog about various ramen the author has tried in Japan. You can also visit his Youtube channel for his ramen videos.
Raymond's Sushi Adventures Local Vancouver blog ran by an avid sushi lover Raymond who posts about many sushi restaurants in Vancouver.
Cooking with Dog's Youtube Channel A youtube channel that focuses on producing recipe videos for home-cook style Japanese comfort food. In my opinion a very educational and interesting channel to learn new cooking skills as well as acquiring various tips and tricks to make Japanese food.
Mark Wien's Youtube Channel Another youtube channel that focuses on the youtuber's the eating experiences, from not only Japan but also Middle East, Germany, Thailand and more. His eating expression is something to see!
Food Sake Tokyo A bilingual Japanese couple's blog about their food experiences in Japan. Here you can see more traditional Japanesefood being enjoyed by locals and food speciaties from different regions of Japan.
